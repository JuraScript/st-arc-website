ST Arc — About Us (O nama)

WHO WE ARE
ST Arc is a specialized manufacturer of custom stainless steel light installations. We operate our own production facilities in Split and Solin, Croatia, giving us complete control over design and manufacturing processes.

MISSION & VALUES
We believe that light has the power to transform how people experience spaces and create memorable moments. Our core philosophy centers on crafting experiences people remember through innovative lighting solutions.

"Vjerujemo da svjetlost ima moć mijenjati iskustvo prostora i stvarati nezaboravne trenutke."
(We believe light has the power to change the experience of space and create unforgettable moments)

COMPANY PHILOSOPHY
"Sa svjetlost stvaramo iskustva koja se pamte"
(Through light we create memorable experiences)

HISTORY
Founded in 1993 in Split, Croatia, ST Arc evolved from a small workshop into a regional leader in custom light installations. Over three decades, they've grown project by project, gradually becoming the company behind iconic seasonal and commercial lighting displays throughout Croatia and Europe.

TRACK RECORD
- 30+ years of continuous operation since 1993
- 200+ completed projects across diverse sectors
- Operations in 12+ European countries
- EU certified products and processes
- Seasonal collaborations with major Croatian cities

WHAT WE DO
ST Arc is a complete solution provider for custom light installations. We design, manufacture, install, and maintain custom stainless steel light installations. We prioritize precision, custom design, durability, and innovation.
