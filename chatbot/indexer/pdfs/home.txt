ST Arc — Home Page

ST Arc specializes in custom stainless steel light installations for cities, municipalities, commercial spaces, events, and private clients.

COMPANY HEADLINE
"Light that transforms spaces" — the company designs, manufactures, and installs large-scale custom inox lighting structures.

KEY MESSAGE
We believe light has the power to change how we experience space and create unforgettable moments. Through innovative lighting solutions, we create memorable experiences that people remember.

COMPANY BACKGROUND
Founded in 1993 in Split, Croatia, ST Arc evolved from a small workshop into a regional leader in custom light installations. Over three decades, they've grown project by project, gradually becoming the company behind iconic seasonal and commercial lighting displays throughout Croatia and Europe.

EXPERIENCE & REACH
- 30+ years of experience in light installations
- 200+ completed projects
- Operations in 12+ European countries
- EU certified products
- Own production workshops in Split and Solin, Croatia
- In-house production capabilities for complete customization
