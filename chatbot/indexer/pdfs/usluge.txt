ST Arc — Services (Usluge)

SERVICE 1: SALES & PROCUREMENT OF DECORATIONS
ST Arc provides a wide selection of LED light decorations, lamps, light tubes, strips, and finished motifs for various project needs.

SERVICE 2: CONSULTATION & CONCEPT DEVELOPMENT
Initial consultation process focused on understanding client vision, space characteristics, target audience, and budget. The team proposes solutions combining aesthetic and financial considerations based on 30 years of experience.

SERVICE 3: SPACE VISUALIZATION & SIMULATION
Uses advanced AI visualization tools to show realistic previews of decorations in actual locations before production. Clients can test different color variations and layouts on photographs of their space.

SERVICE 4: CUSTOM MANUFACTURING
In-house production at their Split and Solin facilities offering fully customized elements—logos, unique motifs, special dimensions, custom shapes, and color combinations. The company controls entire production from sketch to finished product.

Manufacturing Capabilities:
- Fully customized elements
- Custom company logos and brand designs
- Unique motifs and artistic designs
- Special dimensions and non-standard sizes
- Custom shapes (geometric, organic, themed)
- Custom color combinations and finishes
- Bespoke stainless steel constructions
- High-quality stainless steel (corrosion-resistant)
- Modern LED technology integration
- Durable construction designed for years of use

SERVICE 5: INSTALLATION & TECHNICAL SUPPORT
Professional on-site installation with electrical setup and security measures. Ongoing seasonal technical support for repairs and replacements throughout the usage period. Available 24 hours during peak seasons.

SERVICE 6: RENTAL & STORAGE SERVICES
Seasonal rental option including delivery, installation, removal, and return. Includes maintenance and storage between seasons with inspections and repairs as needed.

Rental Includes:
- Delivery to client location
- Professional installation setup
- All necessary electrical connections
- Usage throughout agreed season
- On-call technical support
- Professional removal and takedown
- Transportation back to storage
- Climate-controlled storage facilities
- Regular inspections of stored units
- Maintenance and repair as needed
